Our Story

ARN was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to empower women artisans around the world by providing a platform to showcase their incredible handmade creations.

What began as a small collective of five women has grown into an international community of over 200 artisans from 30+ countries. Each piece in our collection tells a story of tradition, skill, and female entrepreneurship.

We believe in fair trade practices, sustainable materials, and creating opportunities for women to achieve financial independence through their craft.
